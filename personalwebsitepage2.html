INFO ABOUT THE SCHOLARSHIP

This Scholarship was birthed out of a necessity to assist students achieve their goals; students who are not financially stable and cannot complete their education without assistance are number one priority in this scheme and are given preference.

Eligibility

  • Must be a Nigerian studying at any Federal University
  • Must have at least 6 Credits in his/her O'level in one sitting
  • Must have a CGPA of at least 3.0
